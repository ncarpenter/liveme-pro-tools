Tips

Alt + ← Rotate video backward by 90 degrees
Alt + → Rotate video forward by 90 degrees
0 to 9 Seek from 0% to 90% respectively
C Toggle replay chat history messages.
A Seek backward by 10 seconds
D Seek forward by 10 seconds
F Toggle fullscreen
L Toggle loop
M Toggle mute
Space or K Toggle playback
Comma Seek backward by one frame (when paused)
Period Seek forward by one frame (when paused)
← Seek backward by 1 second
→ Seek forward by 1 second
↑ Increase volume
↓ Decrease volume
